**AI/Machine Learning at Duke Electrical and Computer Engineering**

**Overview**

Duke ECE is renowned for its contributions to AI/ML research, ranking among the world's top universities in this field. Our faculty are leading experts in the areas of:

* **Autonomous systems**
* **Intelligent computing systems**
* **Neuromorphic computing**
* **Secure, robust, and explainable machine learning**
* **Federated machine learning and AIoT**
* **Performance modeling**
* **Data modeling**
* **Interpretable predictive models**
* **Computer vision**
* **Biomedical imaging**
