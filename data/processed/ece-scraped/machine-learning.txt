**Machine Learning/Big Data Track**
**Duke Electrical and Computer Engineering**

**Overview**
Duke ECE's Machine Learning/Big Data Track equips master's students with the skills to manage, interpret, and extract insights from data.

**Key Features**
* Ranked among the top 10 in the world in AI/machine learning research.
* Grounding in mathematical foundations of Big Data.
* Practical programming training.
* Instruction in machine learning, statistics, and information theory.
* Vibrant community with cross-campus initiatives in data science and machine learning.

**Degree Options**
* Master of Science (MS) (research-oriented)
* Master of Engineering (MEng) (industry-focused)
* Master of Engineering Management
* Degrees in biomedical, civil/environmental, and risk engineering

**Key Courses**
* ECE 551: Programming, Data Structures, and Algorithms in C++
* ECE 580: Introduction to Machine Learning
* ECE 581: Random Signals and Noise
* ECE 586: Vector Space Methods with Applications
* ECE 590: Various Application Courses
* ECE 682D: Probabilistic Machine Learning
* ECE 685D: Deep Learning
* ECE 687D: Theory of Machine Learning

**Other Notable Courses**
* ECE 555: Probability for ECE
* ECE 585: Signal Detection and Extraction Theory
* ECE 587: Information Theory
* ECE 588: Image and Video Processing
* ECE 651: Software Engineering
* ECE 661: CE ML and Deep Neural Nets
* ECE 684: Natural Language Processing
* Courses from other departments in Engineering or Sciences (CEE, ME, BME, STA, CS, etc)
