Submission Deadline: Envisioning the Invisible Image Contest
Duke Electrical and Computer Engineering

Friday, March 1, 2024
11:55PM

Presenter
Duke Engineering Graduate Student Council

The Engineering Graduate Student Council is proud to present the 2024 edition of Envisioning the Invisible- an image contest that explores the world around us and showcases exciting research and projects across our Duke Engineering community.

All Pratt students, staff and faculty are invited to submit images for consideration by our panel of esteemed judges. Prizes include $200 for each of three Judge's Choice Awards and $200 for an image chosen by the audience! Certain restrictions apply; please see the entry form for details.

To participate, your entry must be submitted before 11:59 pm ET on Friday, March 1, 2024.
