**Academic Curricular Groups**

**Computer Engineering**

* Innovating in computing ecosystem
* Research areas include:
    * Cyber-physical systems
    * Dependability
    * Networking
    * Machine learning
    * Mobile computing
    * Novel computing devices
    * Power-efficient computing
    * Security

**Engineering Physics**

* Leaders in information physics research
* Research areas include:
    * Metamaterials
    * Quantum devices
    * Optical systems
    * Electromagnetics
    * Quantum mechanics

**Microelectronics, Photonics and Nanotechnology**

* Highly vertically integrated research area
* Research areas include:
    * Microfluidic systems
    * Nanoelectronics
    * Optoelectronics
    * Integrated optics
    * Sensors
    * Energy conversion devices
    * Quantum sensors

**Signal and Information Processing**

* Collaboration between ECE, statistics, and applied mathematics
* Research areas include:
    * Defense applications
    * Biomedical applications
    * Wireless networking
    * Embedded systems
    * Machine learning
    * Radar and sonar systems
