**Research Interests**

* Theory, simulation and demonstration of novel electromagnetic metamaterials across the spectrum, from microwave through optical.
* Applications of metamaterials for antennas and imaging devices, with a focus on security imaging and satellite imaging.
* General studies on advanced metamaterials and plasmonics, including nonlinear optics, enhanced spontaneous emission, negative refraction, transformation optics, electromagnetic cloaking, and many other topics
