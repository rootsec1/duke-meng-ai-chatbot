**Hisham Z. Massoud**

- Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Duke University
- Founding director of the Semiconductor Research Laboratory
- Lifetime Fellow of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and Electrochemical Society
- 2006 Electronics and Photonics Division Award of the Electrochemical Society for his work on ultrathin MOS gate dielectric films

**Research Interests:**
- Ultrathin gate dielectrics for CMOS ULSI – the technology, physics, modeling, simulation, and characterization of ultrathin-oxide MOSFETs

**Awards, Honors, and Distinctions:**
- Life Fellow, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, 2015
- Fellow, Electrochemical Society, 2006
- Electronics and Photonics Division Award, Electrochemical Society, 2006
- Inventor Recognition Award, Semiconductor Research Corporation, 2005
