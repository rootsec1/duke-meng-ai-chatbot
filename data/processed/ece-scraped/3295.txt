## AI Health Virtual Seminar: Predictive modeling with multi-modal health data

### Event Details:

- Date: Wednesday, November 30, 2022
- Time: 4:00PM - 5:00PM

### Presenter:

- Matthew M. Engelhard, Assistant Professor of Biostatistics & Bioinformatics
- Host: Andrew Olson, MPP, Associate Director, Policy Strategy and Solutions for Health Data Science, Duke AI Health

### Summary:

This seminar will delve into the potential of unstructured data, such as clinical notes and images, in enhancing clinical decision-making through predictive modeling. Participants will learn techniques for incorporating features from various modalities into a single, neural network-based predictive model. The approach discussed can be applied to a wide range of multi-modal data sources beyond healthcare.
