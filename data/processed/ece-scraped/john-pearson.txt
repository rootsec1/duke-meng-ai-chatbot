**John Pearson**

**Assistant Professor of Neurobiology**

**Research Interests**

* Application of machine learning methods to the analysis of brain data and behavior
* The process by which organisms like songbirds learn complex motor skills without external reinforcement
* Simple information processing principles that can explain the organization of early sensory systems
* Designing new computational methods to analyze neural data and change experiments in real time.
