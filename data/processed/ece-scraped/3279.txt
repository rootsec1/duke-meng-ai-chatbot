**Working with DOD Funding Agencies: A Program Officer's Perspective**

**Date:** Tuesday, September 27, 2022

**Time:** 3:00 PM to 4:00 PM

**Presenter:**

Dr. Kenneth Caster, Program Officer, Organic Materials Chemistry, Air Force Office of Scientific Research

**Abstract:**

This presentation will provide insights on creating competitive proposals for U.S. Department of Defense (DOD) funding.

**Topics:**

- Broad Agency Announcement (BAA)
- White papers
- Proposal submission
- Funding timelines
- Program reviews
- Reports
- Domestic and international collaboration
- Communications

**About the Speaker:**

Dr. Kenneth Caster is a Program Officer for Organic Materials Chemistry at the Air Force Office of Scientific Research. He has a bachelor's degree in chemistry from Stetson University and a doctorate in organic chemistry from Duke University. He has held positions at the University of Florida, Union Carbide Corporation, Lord Corporation, Duke's Center for Biologically Inspired Materials and Material Systems, the U.S. Army Research Office (ARO), and the Air Force Office of Scientific Research (AFOSR).
