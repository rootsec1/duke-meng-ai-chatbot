## For Undergraduates: Summer Research Opportunities in STEM

**Event Details:**

* Date: Friday, November 4, 2022
* Time: 12:00PM - 1:00PM

**Description:**

* College students can get paid to participate in STEM research during the summer.

**Agenda:**

* Topics covered will include:

    * When application season starts
    * What these experiences are like
* Q&A segment for students to ask additional questions
