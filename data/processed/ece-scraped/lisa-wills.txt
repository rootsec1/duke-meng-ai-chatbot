**Research Interests**

* Solving difficult problems surrounding computations to process large volumes of data efficiently.
* Research interests include computer architecture and microarchitecture, hardware-software co-designs, accelerators, and emerging application domains in big data analytics such as genomics, graphs, and databases.
* Goal is to simplify the design, deployment, and usage of custom hardware and leverage hardware acceleration to advance research in the healthcare domain and other natural sciences.
