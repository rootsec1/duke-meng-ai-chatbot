**About the Speaker:**

Dr. Harry Atwater is a Professor of Applied Physics and Materials Science at the California Institute of Technology (Caltech), where he is also the Director of the Resnick Sustainability Institute. He is a world-renowned expert in the field of nanophotonics and metamaterials.

**Research Interests:**

Dr. Atwater's research interests include:

* Metamaterials and plasmonics
* Nanostructured materials for solar energy conversion and photocatalysis
* Semiconductor nanocrystals for light-emitting diodes and lasers
* Advanced microscopy and imaging techniques

**Awards and Honors:**

Dr. Atwater has received numerous awards and honors for his research, including:

* The National Medal of Science
* The IEEE Medal of Honor
* The Materials Research Society Medal
* The MRS Medal for Interdisciplinary Research
