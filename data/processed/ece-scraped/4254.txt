SRC WORKSHOP

February 15, 2024
1:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Wilkinson 132

Presenter: 
Dr. Adam Knapp, John Oakley, and Kashyap Yellai

This workshop will feature three representatives from the Semiconductor Research Corporation (SRC):

•	Dr. Adam C. Knapp (Program Manager for JUMP & JUMP 2.0)
•	John Oakley (Science Director for Hardware Security and AI Hardware), and
•	Kashyap Yellai (Global Research Collaboration Program Manager).

Our guests will discuss:
•	the breadth of the research and various programs that SRC supports
•	the application process
•	strategies to enhance engagement with leading industry members
