**ECE Seminar**

**Technology Enablers that Advance 21st Century Applications in mm-Wave Communications and Nanomedicine**

**Presenter:**

* Rhonda Franklin, Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Minnesota

**Date and Time:**

* Friday, October 30, 2020
* 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM

**Overview:**

This talk will highlight research focused on enabling communications at mm-waves and devices in nanomedicine, with an emphasis on low-power solutions and signal enhancement methods.

**Specific Topics:**

**Communications:**

* Novel low loss design concepts for virtual antenna arrays for phased arrays
* Vertical free-space interconnects for 3D system integration

**Nanomedicine:**

* Concepts for FMR-ID nanolabels based on ferromagnetic resonance (FMR) frequency characterization of magnetic nanowires
* Signal enhancement methods for use as bio-markers in cancer detection
