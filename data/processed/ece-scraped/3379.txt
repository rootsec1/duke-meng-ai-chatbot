**AI Health Virtual Seminar**

**Title:** What are Informatics and Data Science, and Why Do They Matter in Health?

**Date:** Tuesday, March 28, 2023

**Time:** 12:00PM - 1:00PM

**Presenter:**

* W. Edward Hammond, PhD: Professor in Family Medicine and Community Health, Research Professor in the School of Nursing, Professor of Biostatistics and Bioinformatics
* Warren Kibbe, PhD: Professor in Biostatistics & Bioinformatics, Chief for Translational Biomedical Informatics, Chief Data Officer for the Duke Cancer Institute
* Shelley Rusincovitch, MMCi: Managing Director, Duke AI Health

**Description:**

In this one-hour virtual seminar, experts will discuss the foundations of informatics and data science in clinical research and healthcare.

Topics include:

* Challenges and opportunities in data infrastructure
* Future and direction of the HL7® FHIR® standard
* Evolution and future of informatics and data science

**About the Presenters:**

* Dr. Hammond is a pioneer in medical informatics and a founding member of HL7®.
* Dr. Kibbe is Chief for Translational Biomedical Informatics and Chief Data Officer for the Duke Cancer Institute.
* Shelley Rusincovitch is Managing Director of Duke AI Health.
